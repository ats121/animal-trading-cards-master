Black Mamba

Dispite the name, the skin of the black mamba is not black, but it describes the inside of its mouth that is displayed when threatened.

  • Scientific Name: Dendroaspis polylepis
  • Average Length: 8.2 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 11 years
  • Habitat: sub-Saharan Africa

Black mambas live in the savannas and rocky hills of southern and eastern Africa. They are teh largest venomous snake on teh continent. They can reach up to 14 feet in length, but 8.2 feet is the average. They can move at speeds of 12.5 miles per hour, making them among the fastest snakes in the world.
